Welcome to TTT!

TTT is a companion app for playing a real-life game of Trouble in Terrorist Town with your friends. Simply join a friend's room, wait for everyone else to join, and press play!

NOTE:

TTT requires location services to be enabled in order to show nearby rooms. Please enable it in your settings if you haven't already done so.

How to Play

Before the traitor is determined, players are to disperse among the game area. Once all other players are out of sight, determine the round's traitor. The round ends when the Traitor or all of the Innocents are eliminated.

as Traitor:

Your role is to assassinate all of the other players. It is best to keep your role a secret until the best time to strike has arrived.

as Innocent:

You must determine and eliminate the traitor. Be careful! It could be anyone!

Note

Most of this game requires real-life interaction. This app simply assigns the roles of the players at the beginning. The specific rules for the game are for you to decide.
